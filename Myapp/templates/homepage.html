Our Story

A Gurukul Born from Pure Devotion

10+ Years of Seva

Preserving the Sacred Flame of Sanatan Knowledge

Devotional Gurukulam was founded with a singular vision — to make the timeless teachings of the Vedas, Upanishads, and Bhakti tradition accessible to every sincere seeker. Our Gurukul is not merely a school; it is a spiritual home.

Every class, every discourse is offered as a seva — a sacred service to the Almighty and to the community of devotees seeking liberation through knowledge and devotion.
